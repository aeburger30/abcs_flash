Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Overview

"ABCs in a Flash!" (the App) is an educational tool designed for children. We respect the privacy of children and families. This policy describes the information we collect and how it is used.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers are requested.
  • No location data is tracked.
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs are collected.

3. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for families and is compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

4. External Links

Any external links (such as to our support page) are protected by a "Parental Gate" (a challenge question) to ensure only adults can access them.
